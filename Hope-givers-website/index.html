Welcome to Hope Givers Initiative

Giving hope to the less priviledged.

We are a startup organization founded in January 2024

MISSION

To give hope to the less privileged and end early marriages in Uganda.

"We aim to provide hope to the less privileged and end early marriages in Uganda through empowerment, education, and community engagement."

Our initiative was inspired by the urgent need to make a difference in our community, which is plagued by early marriages that steal dreams from victims. We believe that by providing hope to those in need, we can create a better world for us all.

Hope Givers Initiative is led by Bill Cheptoyek, a student at Makerere University. Bill is committed to being the change that our society needs and is dedicated to making a positive impact on the next generation.

Community Engagement

Strategies for engaging with the general community and addressing early child marriages and poverty

*Collaborating with local leaders, schools, and religious institutions to promote awareness and advocate for change. *Organizing community forums or town hall meetings to facilitate dialogue and gather input from community members. *Implementing culturally sensitive approaches to address social norms and practices contributing to early marriages.
